Execution really shapes whether your company takes off or not.

– Pete Cashmore, Mashable

Apple has a culture of excellence that is, I think, so unique and so special. I'm not going to witness or permit the change of it.

– Tim Cook, Apple

I use Facebook all the time. I'm not a believer that they're going to do everything on the Internet better than anyone else.

– Dennis Crowley, Foursquare

I've found that if you're not responsive to e-mail, it trains people to leave you alone.

-David Karp, Tumblr

The social network is the paradigm of the modern service application.

– Larry Ellison , Oracle

The best customer service is if the customer doesn't need to call you, doesn't need to talk to you. It just works.

– Jeff Bezos, Amazon.com

You have to maintain a culture of transformation and stay true to your values.

– Jeff Weiner, Linkedin

Well, today people have to be self-reliant if they want a secure retirement income.

– Scott Cook, Intuit

You must always be able to predict what's next and then have the flexibility to evolve.

– Marc Benioff,  SalesForce.com
